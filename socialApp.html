Social Media App

Full stack web application that uses the MERN stack. Users can create a post for everyone can see by filling in the form. The post form uses redux form. As soon as user posts, it'll appear in the list of posts, with the title, and the amount of comments on that post. Leaving a comment is easy, just fill in the comment form and it'll appear on the list of comments. Users can also delete their posts and comments, for every post and comment user creates, they'll have the "delete" button appear.
