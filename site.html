Artificial Sweetener Cancer or Myth?

What are the claims?

The claim is that consuming artificial sweetener can give you cancer. Why so?
Artificial sweetener contains chemicals like sucralose or asparatame that are meant to replicate the same taste of regular sugar without actually gaining the calories.

Typically, a can or bottle of soda will contain around 100 calories and 40-80 grams of sugar. In one serving of soda, you will have exceeded your recommended daily sugar intake (which should be less than 40). Lots of soda brands have versions of their drink like diet coke and coke zero. In addition, there are many brands that sell artificial sweetener in little packets. Access to artificial sugar is as easy as regular sugar. A lot of stores sell them. Additionally, there is a lot of advertisements going towards them, such as weight loss and healthy eating programs.
