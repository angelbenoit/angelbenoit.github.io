Study Session

Full stack application where users can schedule their study sessions. With the built in calendar, users can pick any day (not past dates) and schedule what subjects to study and for how long. If there is anything scheduled for the current day, users can check the "Start your session" page and users can start the timer to start timing your study session. Once user finishes studying everything for the day, the page will inform you that you're completed. There is a graph in the overview page showing your progress.
