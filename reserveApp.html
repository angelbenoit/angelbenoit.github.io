Bart App

Full stack web app using the MERN Stack. Users will be able to view the menu and then reserve. After picking a time and date, user will then be able to pick the meals for the reservation. Once the user picked out all the meals, they will be able to view the confirmation modal to see the total price and details. In addition, users will also be able to view history of all the reservations they've made. They can also cancel or remove items from their history.
